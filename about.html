About me

I am Riccardo Di Girolamo, a soon-to-graduate Master's Student in Mechanical Engineering at TUDelft, with a background in Aerospace Engineering from Politecnico di Milano. This summer, I am thrilled to be participating in Google Summer of Code 2024, where I will be contributing to the SymPy project. My work will focus on Developing and Benchmarking Equations of Motion Generation Methods in the physics.mechanics module. Through this website and accompanying blog, I aim to share updates and insights on my progress and experiences throughout the project
